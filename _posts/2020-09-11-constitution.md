Every country has a constitution. 
A constitution is an aggregate of fundamental principles or established precedents that constitute the legal basis of a polity, 
organisation or other type of entity and commonly determine how that entity is to be governed (this definitely is from wikipedia).

So, have you ever thought of having one yourself? 
A personal constitution. Actually you can. I'm pretty sure no one else knows about you as much as you do. 
So, why not write one yourself? 
It is supposed to be the unchangeable documentation that you must follow at any cost so as to continuously bring out the better version of yourself.

Well, the articles in a constitution are grouped into parts starting with the Preamble. 
A Preamble is like a preliminary or introductory statement. 
It can be something you'd want to write that makes you follow them. 
However you are gonna want to be in your best mind to write the whole thing though. Write it up such that it doesn't need to be changed.

Does it make a difference in your life? Yes, it absolutely does. It makes you rethink when you do something out of the law. 
I mean out of your law. The law you make for yourself to follow. 
You can be telling yourself many a times about getting better, being productive, spending time with your family, having a good time, etc. 
But is it something you'd follow absolutely? If you did, you would not be telling yourself those words everytime. 
Yes, you might be doing all of those now itself, but at some point, you just snap. Who wouldn't? 
But, don't you wish to have something that could get you out of that? That makes you not do that? Keeps a check on you? 
Like a police officer who could arrest you when you break the law? 
How about you make a government for yourself with a constitution, rule book, etc. (whatever you might call it) where you are the sole person responsible 
for all the crimes and all the good things you have done in your life? Wouldn't that bring the best out of yourself? Pretty sure it does. What makes to

If you write one for yourself, a personal constitution, you must promise yourself about not breaking the law you made under any circumstances. 
Whatever critical decision you are making, be it stone cold or the opposite(I don't know the term for that), you must never ever break your law. 
This is what defines you. This is what makes you understand what you truly are. This is what you have to be. 
Because, if you don't, you'll regret the decision you made later. This is what helps you everytime for the rest of your life. 
I don't say that you follow the same rules forever in your life. Everything changes. You do. 
But when you make a change in the constitution, let it be because of the change in your life, but not because of one of your instantaneous decisions that 
you want to suddenly want to party tonight and get wasted.

Also, whenever you get stuck at a decision, be it anything, you can decide by this because it defines you. 
You can find what you can do when you lose yourself. It is what you are.

If you are thinking how to start, you can follow a few steps below that can help you.
- Think about all the different categories in your life. Everything. Career, family, social life, entertainment, health, lifestyle, etc.
- Be sure you have all the categories that you have in your life that you want to govern.
- Create them as sections. A section doesn't need to include subsections and all unless you need to. It can also consist of only one sentence that can govern the whole category itself such that it doesn't need to be changed for the environment you live right now. You can have each section describing how you want to live your life of that category. It doesn't need to be much. It just needs to be good enough for you to follow.
- Now, write yourself a preamble like "_I, ABC, am writing this constitution to ....._".
- The most important part is that you never forget to follow this. This is what guides you in your life.
