You may have come across a lot of people in your life that you can classify as weird, but are they really that? What actually do you think is weird? 
Is it their appearance (like dressing style, etc)? Is it their behaviour? Do you hate that? Did that make you feel uncomfortable? 
Or is it just that you can talk about them being something or someone who doesn't care what others think or do with what they are?
